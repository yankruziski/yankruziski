## About me

Started in financial markets at **15** at [High Trading](https://www.genialinvestimentos.com.br/) — a holding of Genial Investimentos with a strong quant and fundamentalist approach. That's where I found my passion for **quantitative finance, mathematical models, and data analysis**.

Currently studying **Business & Technology** at [Inteli — Instituto de Tecnologia e Liderança](https://www.inteli.edu.br/), where project-based learning bridges Computing, Business, and Leadership.

I'm drawn to the frontier between **Quant Finance** and **Quantum Computing** — where mathematical rigor meets next-generation computing paradigms that will reshape how we model risk, price derivatives, and optimize portfolios.

- Completed the **Morgan Stanley** program
- Member of **Mensa International**
- Organized a talk with [Mauro Shinzato](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mauro-shinzato/) from **Giant Steps Capital** (largest quant fund in Latin America) to bridge students into the quant world
